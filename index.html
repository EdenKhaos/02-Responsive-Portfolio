About Me

My name is Heather, and my background is in building architecture and interior design. However, I love working with graphics and am a self-taught graphic designer and amateur photographer. I love video games and the design that goes into them and hope to one day be programing and modeling the environment of a game. Of course, I enjoy cinematography and the music that is part of the movies in which I've avidly followed for years.


My hobbies include anything to spent time in the outdoors such as skiing, hiking, archery, rafting, cycling, camping, and overall just being in the mountains. I'm engaged and hope to be married sometime next year. My fiance and I added a puppy to our family almost a year ago who brings us much joy to have around on our adventures. I'm originally from Portland Or but lived in Arizona, Nevada, and now Colorado. I've traveled the most outside of the U.S. to eleven countries and hope to continue to do so in the near future. My favorite countries so far are Norway, Switzerland, Japan, and Netherlands.
